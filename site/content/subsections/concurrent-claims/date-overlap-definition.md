## Three conditions are required to catch all overlapping claims
id: date-overlap-rule
type: remember

When identifying claims that are concurrent with an event of interest, capture any claim whose date range overlaps OR encompasses the event dates — not just claims that start within the event window.

[compare]
wrong: date-overlap-wrong | Incorrect — too narrow, misses spanning claims
correct: date-overlap-correct | Correct — three-condition overlap
[/compare]

[callout tip "Example"]
Event: Jan 15 – Jan 15. Concurrent facility claim: Jan 14 – Jan 16. The correct logic catches this; the incorrect logic misses it because the claim starts before the event.
[/callout]
