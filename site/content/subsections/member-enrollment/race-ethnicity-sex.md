## Stack, classify, and deduplicate race and ethnicity across years
id: demographics-snippet
type: code
snippet: race-ethnicity-sex

Stacks member records across years, classifies race and ethnicity using ME021 and ME025 codes, replaces MCD QHP race/ethnicity with HCIP values where available, then rank-deduplicates by studyid to produce a single cross-year demographics reference table.
