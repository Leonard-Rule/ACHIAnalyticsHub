**Stat** — headline data figure in IBM Plex Mono with a label and optional source. The core of ACHI's data-forward storytelling.

```jsx
<Stat value="1 in 3" label="Arkansans have some form of Medicaid coverage" source="ACHI, 2024" />
<Stat value="44%" label="of rural labor & delivery units at risk of closing" color="maroon" size="lg" />
```

Props: `size` (sm/md/lg), `color` (navy/teal/maroon/inverse).
