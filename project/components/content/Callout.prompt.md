**Callout** — inline note/advisory box with a tone icon and left accent bar. Use for data caveats, key takeaways, methodology notes.

```jsx
<Callout tone="brand" title="Key takeaway">
  Mothers in some parts of Arkansas had C-sections at much higher rates than others.
</Callout>
<Callout tone="warning">Data for 2024 is preliminary and subject to revision.</Callout>
```

Tones: `info` · `success` · `warning` · `danger` · `brand`.
