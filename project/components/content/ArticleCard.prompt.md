**ArticleCard** — the publication/blog card used across ACHI listings: cover image (teal→navy placeholder if none), topic tag, mono date line, title, excerpt. Becomes a link with `href`.

```jsx
<ArticleCard
  category="Maternal & Infant Health" categoryColor="teal"
  meta="June 24, 2026 · Publication"
  title="C-Section Rates in Arkansas, 2021–2023"
  excerpt="Mothers in some parts of the state had the procedure at much higher rates than others."
  href="/publications/c-section-rates"
/>
```
