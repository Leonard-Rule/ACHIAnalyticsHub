**SectionHeading** — the signature ACHI section header: uppercase teal eyebrow, short accent rule, display title, optional description. Use to open any page section.

```jsx
<SectionHeading
  eyebrow="Publications"
  title="Travel time to delivery facilities for Arkansas mothers"
  description="Longer travel times are associated with higher risks of adverse maternal and neonatal outcomes."
/>
<SectionHeading eyebrow="Our priorities" title="Healthier Arkansas" align="center" size="xl" />
```

Props: `size` (md/lg/xl), `align` (left/center), `inverse` (dark bg), `rule` (toggle accent rule).
