**Tag** — rounded pill for topics & categories (e.g. "Arkansas Medicaid", "Maternal & Infant Health"). Clickable when given `href`.

```jsx
<Tag color="teal">Maternal & Infant Health</Tag>
<Tag color="navy" href="/arkansas-medicaid/">Arkansas Medicaid</Tag>
<Tag color="neutral">Blog</Tag>
```

Colors: `neutral` · `teal` · `navy` · `maroon` · `solid` · `outline`.
